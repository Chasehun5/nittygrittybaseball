Nitty Gritty Baseball

We’re a Springville, Alabama youth baseball organization built on hard work, humility, and heart. Nitty Gritty Baseball gives kids a positive place to grow—refining fundamentals, building confidence, and learning how to handle success and adversity. With experienced coaches and a supportive community, we strive to make every practice purposeful, every game meaningful, and every season memorable.
